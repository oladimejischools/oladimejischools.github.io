About Us

Oladimeji International School is founded to teach children the right knowledge based upon the right foundation. The focus is to give young children, boys and girls sound education. They will grow up to stand out tall, as adults who are not only academically sound, but also sound in body, spirit and mind, and thus fulfil the purpose of man on the planeth earth.
